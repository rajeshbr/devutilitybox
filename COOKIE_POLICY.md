## 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit a website. They serve various purposes:

- **Remembering preferences** (e.g., dark mode setting)
- **Maintaining sessions** (keeping you logged in)
- **Analyzing usage** (understanding how users interact with the site)
- **Security** (preventing fraud and unauthorized access)

**Types of Cookies:**
- **Session Cookies:** Deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent Cookies:** Remain on your device for a set period
- **First-Party Cookies:** Set by DevUtilityBox
- **Third-Party Cookies:** Set by external services (currently not used)

You can manage cookies using your browser settings:

### Chrome/Edge/Brave

2. Manage exceptions or clear all cookies

## 15. FAQ

**Q: Do I need to accept all cookies?**  
A: No. Only essential cookies are required. You can reject optional cookies without losing functionality.
